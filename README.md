# Vanguard Veterans Consulting Website

Official website for **Vanguard Veterans Consulting** — a veteran-focused consulting business that helps clients build strong, fully developed VA disability claims through education, strategy, and medical records review.

---

## 🇺🇸 About the Business

Vanguard Veterans Consulting provides **consulting and educational services only**.  
We do not file claims or represent veterans before the VA.

Our focus is to help veterans:
- Understand the VA claims process
- Identify strong claimable conditions
- Develop Fully Developed Claims (FDC)
- Strengthen evidence through medical records review
- Avoid common claim mistakes

---

## 💼 Services Offered

### New Claims (FDC Premium)
- Full medical records review
- Claim strategy development
- Evidence gap analysis
- Pre-submission audit

### Supplemental Claims
- Denial analysis
- New evidence strategy
- Claim restructuring guidance

### Appeals
- Advanced case strategy
- Appeal lane selection guidance
- Evidence and argument structuring
